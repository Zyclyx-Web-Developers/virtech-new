End User Computing

Configurations designed to enhance productivity, improve processes and reinforce security

End-user computing refers to consolidating individual systems required to accomplish the daily workload into a single platform, delivering a more efficient and holistic user experience. We try to provide the same by developing an intuitive, agile, scalable and manageable platform. As a team, our initiative is to ensure the users and managers can utilize the platform without system expertise ensuring a fluid introduction of new technologies to end-users.

Improve productivity, processes and security

Secured Virtual Client Solutions (VDI)

The end-users gain remote access to their virtual desktops with our Secured VDI solutions without compromising on network security. Our process involves design, installation, training and support services for Secured Virtual clients and Virtual Desktop infrastructures.

Custom Device Solutions

Our solutions are customized to ensure that the design, engineering, and deployment meets with every organization's unique needs. We believe when any device is custom made it has greater potential to give transformational results.

Mobile Solutions

At VIRTECH, we offer key mobile solutions in the form of Desktop Virtualization and Secure remote data access. Our team works towards providing more efficient use of computing resources along with maintaining its security.

Enablement Solutions

Our solutions are dedicated to improving the overall experience of the end-user by empowering them with an “always-on” access to documents, videos, and other critical data without compromising security.
